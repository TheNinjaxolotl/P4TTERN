HOW TO PLAY
1
Watch the tiles light up in a sequence. Pay close attention to the order!
2
Repeat the sequence by tapping the tiles in the exact same order.
3
Each round adds one more step to the sequence. How far can you go?
4
Earn gems for every correct tap. The gem rate increases as you level up!
5
Spend gems in the Shop on items like Revival Tokens to continue after a mistake.
TIP: GEMS EARNED PER TAP INCREASES EVERY 5 LEVELS.
HIGHER LEVELS = BIGGER REWARDS.
